My bio from LinkedIn

Hi, my name is Ian “Gerard” Shaughnessy; I am born and raised in St. Louis City. I have military, sales, and photography backgrounds and I have been a professional photographer for over 5 years. I am an aspiring full-stack developer at Savvy Coders and I have a Bachelor's in Entrepreneurship from Webster University. Growing up, I always knew I wanted to be in the military. I joined the navy with the intention of working on submarines and doing the work nobody wanted to do. My service ended early due to medical complications but the lessons I learned have made me into a “battle hardened" worker ready to take on the world.
Currently, I am a photographer. I have had a camera in my hands since I was a kid, the device always fascinated me. I started taking photos for people in 2015, high-school, but did not earn my first dollar with this skill until my sophomore year in college. Photography has taken me all around the midwest; for live events such as concerts and speeches, prominent people in St. Louis and Columbia, real estate, conventions, charities, weddings, engagements, office parties, and of course: Portraits! My claim to fame is working for the Angel Band Project, an absolute beast of a non-profit organization, and when they spoke at the United Nations, one of my photos was used during the speech!
I always intend to have a camera on me, no matter the job I do. I have met the most amazing people in my life through this skill and I intend to do the same thing with computer programming. As I said before, I am aspiring to be a full-stack developer. I want to take my career in a direction where I can work with mobile applications involving cameras; front end or back end. 5-10 years from now, I would like to own my own business where I can take all the things I love and create something that makes the world better. What I am willing to bring to any future employer is discipline, creativity, and a smile into the work place. Because of this, I believe I can impact any business in a positive way.
